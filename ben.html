Ben Dodge

Ben is a first-year Physics Ph.D. student currently rotating in the group. He received a B.A. in Physics from Princeton University, where he worked with Mariangela Lisanti on Milky Way merger dynamics. He is broadly interested in galaxy physics, dark matter, and computational techniques. His rotation project focuses on exploring the use of halo star absorption lines for cold gas tomography in the Milky Way. When he is not learning physics, Ben enjoys walking, making food, and traveling by train.
