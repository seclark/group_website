Laywood Fayne

Laywood Fayne is a junior (Class of 2024) and Engineering Physics major at Stanford. He joined our group in the summer of 2021 and is working on statistically measuring the spatial coherence between diffuse interstellar material and the local magnetic field structure. He is generally interested in space and physics and is a member of the Stanford Student Space Initiative. Laywood is from New York City and also enjoys sports, movies and comics.
