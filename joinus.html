Interested in joining our group?

Prospective postdocs: Anyone interested in a postdoctoral position with our group should plan to apply to the KIPAC Fellowships in the fall. Please also consider applying to the Stanford Science Fellowship (note earlier deadline). I am also happy to host fellows here via Hubble, Jansky, Banting, etc -- please reach out if you plan to apply with Stanford as your host institution. I am happy to host prospective postdoc visitors via Stanford PRISM.

Current and incoming graduate students: Projects are available-- please get in touch!

Prospective Stanford graduate students should apply to the Ph.D. program through the Stanford Physics department. I do plan to take on graduate students from the 2025-26 application cycle.

Current Stanford undergraduate students: Please get in touch if you are interested in research with our group, either during the academic year or over the summer. Undergraduate research at Stanford qualifies for Federal Work Study pay: please reach out to discuss.

Current undergraduate students at other institutions: Summer research positions at Stanford are available through CalBridge Summer and the SR-EIP/Leadership Alliance program.

I do not have research positions for high school students.
