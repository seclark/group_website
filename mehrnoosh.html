Mehrnoosh Tahani

Mehrnoosh Tahani is a Banting Fellow, sponsored by the government of Canada, and a KIPAC fellow. Previously, she was a research associate (Covington Fellow) with the National Research Council Canada at the Dominion Radio Astrophysical Observatory. You can find more about her research on her personal webpage.
