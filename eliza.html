Eliza Gallagher

Eliza Gallagher is a sophomore (Class of 2025) at Stanford. She is currently planning on majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Astronomy. She joined the undergraduate research group in the winter of 2022. She is currently working on researching turbulent diffusion scales in simulated polarized dust emission data. Eliza is originally from Louisville, Kentucky and she enjoys singing, playing the guitar, and playing golf. At Stanford, she is an active member of Women in Computer Science. She is also passionate about getting girls and minorities involved in STEM and does this through the non-profit STEM You Can!, which she is National director of Operations of.
