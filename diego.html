Diego Maglione

Diego Maglione is a junior at Stanford majoring in Physics and Art Practice. He is currently working on developing new software to measure the alignment of galactic density structures with magnetic fields in magnetohydrodynamic simulations of galaxies. Diego is also involved with the Stanford ACLU and the Stanford Storyboard club. In his free time, he enjoys boxing, drawing, and going on bike rides.
