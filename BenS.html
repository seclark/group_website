Ben Sherwin

Ben is a Physics Ph.D. student from Tampa, Florida. Before arriving at Stanford, he attended the University of Florida, where he received a B.S. with majors in Physics, Astrophysics, and Mathematics. Ben is currently using magnetohydronamic simulations to investigate the nature of cosmic ray transport in the interstellar medium. When he is not studying physics, Ben enjoys live theatre, films, and crossword puzzles.
